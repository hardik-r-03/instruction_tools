Objective: Resolve failed transactions or missing purchased items while ensuring safety and transparency.
Tone Focus: Professional, calm, and security-aware.

Instructions:

Thank the player for supporting the game and apologize for the trouble.

Ask for non-sensitive purchase details (approximate date, item name, platform).

Remind them not to share personal payment info.

Suggest checking bank or app-store transaction history to confirm status.

If payment was successful but item not delivered, confirm it will be escalated.

Explain expected resolution timelines clearly.

End with gratitude and reassurance that their payment is safe and being verified.
